FOOD WASTEAGE MANAGEMENT

You have two hands. One to help yourself, the second to help others.

So Food Waste is an organisation which aims to redistribute excess food from weddings, parties, events to those who are hungry.

Vision

To bring a social change in every individual in-order to reduce food waste and to make the World Hunger Free.

Mission

The purpose of Excess food wastage management is to provide food for orphanages , old-age homes & poor peoples who are in need of food .
